### 🩸 Who I am

I study how games create **fear**, **tension**, and **meaning**.  
Not just *what* works — but **why** it works, and how to build it with intention.

My focus is **psychological horror** and **narrative-driven design** — the systems, mechanics, and structural choices that make certain games impossible to forget.

Long-term goal: working at an international game studio. Destination — **Japan** 🗾
